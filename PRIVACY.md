# Privacy Policy for SingleTask

**Effective Date:** January 8, 2026

Kingjo ("we", "our", or "us") operates the SingleTask mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Application.

## 1. Information Collection and Use
We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information or data. 
- **Task Data:** All tasks created within the app are stored locally on your device in a private database. We have no access to your tasks.
- **Audio/Haptics:** The app uses local device features for notifications and feedback, but no data is sent to external servers.

## 2. Permissions
The app requests the following permissions for functional purposes only:
- **Notifications:** To keep the focus timer active in the background.
- **Vibration:** To provide haptic feedback upon task completion.

## 3. Third-Party Services
We do not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.

## 4. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
